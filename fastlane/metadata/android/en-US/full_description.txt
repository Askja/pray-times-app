Prayer Times helps Muslims keep daily prayer times, qibla direction, reminders, Ramadan entries and Islamic calendar dates in one Android app.

The app calculates Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib and Isha using well-known calculation methods. It can choose a method automatically from the selected location, or you can select a method and Asr rule manually.

Main features:

- Daily prayer times with the current prayer highlighted.
- Countdown for the active prayer period and the next prayer.
- Month schedule export to PDF and ICS.
- Qibla compass with direction angle.
- Saved locations, city search and manual map point selection.
- Optional notifications before prayer start, at prayer start and before prayer end.
- Optional adhan at prayer start.
- Per-prayer notification profiles.
- Iqama and jamaat offsets for saved locations.
- Hijri calendar and major Islamic holidays.
- Ramadan mode with suhoor, iftar and tarawih labels.
- Sunnah and nafl prayer windows.
- Home screen widget.
- JSON backup and restore for settings and locations.
- Light and dark themes.
- Multiple languages with RTL support.

Locations and settings are stored locally on the device. Internet access is used for map tiles, address search and mosque lookup.

Prayer times and Hijri dates are calculated values. Local mosques or religious authorities may use regional rules, minute offsets or moon-sighting decisions, so adjust the app when your local schedule differs.
